Learning

December 4, 2025

In the past year I've learned about learning. I've learned that learning and the desire to learn will lead to both personal growth and knowledge creation. I've also learned that learning is not a process that takes place in a coercive environment. Learning is a process which must start from one's own desire and creativity. I've learned that when in that place of desire it's important to take a concept, understand the intuition, ask why, go deeper into that why, and further understand the intuition of the why, all the way down to the bottom, when you have the explanation fully grounded in you like the roots of a tree. Out of this tree something beautiful flowers. Your knowledge can now be applied and related to an infinite numbers of things, regardless of the subject matter. This is learning and creativity. The ability to understand and connect ideas knows no bounds.
